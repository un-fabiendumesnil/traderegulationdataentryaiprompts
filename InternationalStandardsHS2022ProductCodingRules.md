# HS 2022 Product Coding Rules

Identify the affected goods from the [Products description], scope, definitions, and operative provisions of the text.

Use HS 2022 codes at the most specific practical level, normally six-digit subheadings. Do not use a broader heading if the legal text only covers part of that heading, unless no more precise HS 2022 subheading exists. When coverage is partial, clearly state “Partial” and explain the limitation, such as excluding products not covered by the regulation.

When needed, consult an official HS 2022 source, preferably WCO HS 2022 nomenclature or another authoritative customs nomenclature source. Do not invent HS codes. If classification is uncertain, provide the most likely code and clearly explain the uncertainty.

For the affected products in this regulation/standard, use [HS heading or subheading explanation]. Explain briefly why the selected code or codes match the product scope.
